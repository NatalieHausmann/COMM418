<p>Within the data, I found that more people would rather confront 100 duck-sized horses than one horse-sized duck. <p>This appears to be a helpful tool when dealing with large data sets that cannot be easily counted without the use of an additional tool, like a pivot table. The pivot table can then be turned into a chart or visualization that is easier to comprehend. This is a frequently used tool for data journalists as they seek to create information that is digestable to the general public. </p>
